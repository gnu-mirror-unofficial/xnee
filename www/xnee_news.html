Comment:
As Xnee now reds from stdin (if no file specified) when replaying it is possible
to pipe the result from a recording xnee (xnee --record) to a replaying (xnee --replay)
And of course you can use the --distribute option in both of them


Example:
E.g xnee --record --device_event_range 2-8 --display localhost:0 --distribute remotehost:1 | xnee --replay --display somehost:0 --device_event_range 2-8 --distribute otherhost1:0,otherhost2:0

Xnee will then:
- record Events 2 to 8 on display localhost:0
- distribute these (events) to remotehost:1
- print them (events) to stdout. These are piped to xnee --replay
- replay them on somehost:0 (--display option) with synchronisation (synch is default)
- distribute them to otherhost1:0 and otherhost2:0
But I figure it will be slow ;)
